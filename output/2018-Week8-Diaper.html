Week 8 Diaper Pail

Week 8 of the season, week 10 with my daughter, and other than trying to figure out if my daughter is smiling at me or having a muscle spasm in her face, my 6-2 record seems to be something I can always smile about. Nothing wipes a smile off someone's face like a good dirty piece of crap shoved in your face; and which happened this week after dom took fantasy football advice from Jeffrey leading to the closest match we've had since my match up with Brock week 2. We can tease dom for this for as long as it stings for him, but barner's 0.4 points hides the real culprit of his demise... the mighty Doug Baldwin! Whom dom heralded in the draft as a premiere receiver who would dominate the league this year and calling us all Bafoons for passing over him. Dom's first team name was even named after Baldwin win win.... since I'm writing this illegally parked at a bus stop while my wife gets her eyebrows done, this is where we end and I reflect on how I got here in life.... but at least I'm 6-2... good luck to everyone but Lexie this week.

Written by Robert Andruskiewicz in 2018 on Sat 03 November 2018.
