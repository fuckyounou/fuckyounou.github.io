Week 12 Diaper Pail

For some reason, it looks like everyone decided to quit on their final columns. Everyone quits for their own reasons: shame, exhaustion, lack of enthusiasm, among just a few. But this season, we saw one man rise among defeat to keep trying, Meek. A still broken man, Meek would not bend on making season ending trades. Meek could have quit the league at midway like fellow Buffalo Bill Vontae Davis, but instead he saw his fate until the end. Instead, he stayed loyal to AB and Davante Adams, and never bailed on LeSean McCoy. That perhaps was the the biggest fail of them all, holding on too tightly to a washed up veteran. McCoy not only disappointed this week, but all season. He had one respective game against the Jets, but otherwise has barely managed 10 points per game. So our diaper goes to Mr. Lesean McCoy, not just for this week, but for the entire season. Lesean McCoy was a man worth quitting on.... As for the column unless Isaac or Brian lose their first round bye, I can't see there being any point to writing a week 13 column since everything is pretty much in order. We will return for the first week of the playoffs otherwise. Good luck to everyone except Brian, Ike and Meek this week.

Written by Robert Andruskiewicz in 2018 on Thu 29 November 2018.
